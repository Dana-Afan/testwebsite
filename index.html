What Is So Good About Cainta Catholic College?

Below are the reasons why Cainta Catholic College is very pleasant:

Catholic School

Cainta Catholic College is a school where young people can take classes. This is where the majority of a student's lessons and learnings take place. This is a catholic school where religions will eventually be taught. This school has a large number of Filipino students. There is no racist in this building. So come on over and join us!

Large Buildings

Cainta Catholic College has a lot of built space. However, it is very convenient for this school because all rooms for each section are separate. Every section has classrooms set up for students to learn in. This school has many campuses, which is why it is so well built. It also has a nice, open space where students can play.

B.E.C. - K to 12

Cainta Catholic College educates even toddlers and children. They provide a broad education in which students are expected to be responsible and productive. Teachers teach the students, despite the fact that the students being educated are still very young. Teachers are extremely patient. Our School's Campus

Below are the campuses you can find in our school and a brief description about it:

STAGE

Some events take place in the stage area. It has numerous applications. You can stand there and speak when the opportunity arises. If you want, you can also play there. That stage is in front of a large area that is available for playing, practicing, and, most likely, wonderful events.

AUDITORIUM

Many events take place in the auditorium room. The majority of the events take place within this structure. This location has a stage for performing, speaking, and displaying talents. This is where recognition and graduation take place. Other events that take place here include orientation, practice, and many more.

GYMNASIUM

Variety of events take place in the gymnasium, particularly those that do not occur simultaneously. Science month, nutrition month, quiz bee, miting de avance, and many other events are held in the gymnasium. This is also a location for sports such as basketball and badminton.

Our Facilities

Below are the facilities you can find in our school and a brief description about it:

Most Extravagant Parish

Our Lady of Light Parish is the name of this parish. It is a parish designed for people to attend mass, particularly Catholics. Anyone is welcome to attend mass here. There are no fees to pay. If you want to attend a mass, you should go to this parish. If the outside is fascinating, so is the inside. World Class Library

CCC's Library is open to all students. We can read books, magazines, short stories, and other materials there. The Library, like any other library, contains a large number of books. Because of the quietness and cold air in the room, every student can concentrate in this library. The library, on the other hand, is extremely secure. To go on this, you must have a library card. Tasty and Healthy Food

Our Extracurricular Activities

Below are the extracurricular activities you can attend in our school and a brief description about it:

DISCLAIMER: There are many more events and activities at Cainta Catholic College, but these are the most enjoyable.

INTRAMURALS

Intramurals are times when students are legally permitted to participate in sports such as volleyball, basketball, and badminton. Typically, this is done in the month of November. This is similar to a competition in which the goal is to win. However, the goal of intramurals is not only to win, but also to have fun. Even if non-winners do not receive a medal or trophy, what matters is that they gave it their all, and that is what Intramurals are all about.

FOUNDATION DAY

Foundation Day is a time of year when rides, booths, and other games are available. Typically, this is done in the month of February. The students are free to ride the rides and participate in other games in which they are expected to have fun and enjoy themselves. However, you will need a ticket for a specific game. That ticket should be purchased. Additionally, lower grade level students are not permitted to ride without a guardian.

BUWAN NG WIKA

Buwan ng Wika is a time of year when students are encouraged to speak exclusively in Tagalog. It is typically made in the month of August. This is a way to show respect for our country and language while also celebrating and emphasizing the importance of our national language, Filipino. During this month, students are asked to wear traditional costumes such as Filipiniana, Barong, Katipunero, and so on. You can also perform on stage in a speific date given by the school.

NUTRITION MONTH

Nutrition Month is a time of year when students are encouraged to eat or bring nutritious and healthy foods to school. Typically, this is done in the month of July. Their goal is to educate people about the importance of eating nutritious foods and to raise nutrition awareness among Filipinos. This month, students will also be asked to cook or make nutritious foods and other healthy foods on one day. However, not all students will cook or prepare nutritious foods because not all students are capable of doing so.

ECO-FASHION SHOW

The Eco-Fashion Show is a time of year when students design their own dresses or suits out of recyclable materials. This is typically done in the month of November. Their goal is to dress up and stand on stage to show their appreciation for Mother Nature. This is also an event to demonstrate to students how recyclable materials can be used to make things and how reducing the use of recyclable materials can make a difference in the world we live in today.

QUIZ BEES

A Quiz Bee Competition is a smart quiz in which students respond to questions on the active board using the Activote device. A quiz bee competition does not have a set month. The fastest students and top scorers win the competition. It is an arena where the brightest minds compete to see who can score the most points after a certain number of rounds in order to gain some measure of bragging rights and honor. The quiz bee consists of questions from a variety of subjects and topics, including science, mathematics, general knowledge, and information technology.
